My Favorite Fish

The Sra fish is known for its vibrant colors and active nature. It thrives in clean, warm water and requires regular care. Aquarists love this species for its beauty and interactive behavior. Ensure the tank has plants and hiding spots to mimic its natural habitat.

Facts About Sra Fish

  • Origin: Southeast Asia
  • Average lifespan: 5–7 years
  • Diet: Omnivorous
